## What is a Network?
A network is a collection of computers and devices connected together to share resources.

## Key Concepts
- **IP Address** — Unique address for each device
- **Switch** — Connects devices within a LAN
- **Router** — Connects different networks together
- **Firewall** — Protects network from unauthorized access
